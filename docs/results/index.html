Fighting Corporate Greed

Getting Results

DRAFT DRAFT DRAFT DRAFT DRAFT DRAFT DRAFT DRAFT DRAFT DRAFT DRAFT DRAFT DRAFT DRAFT DRAFT DRAFT

For over a decade, Jovanka has been making national headlines for standing up to big corporations like Chevron. She knows what it's like to be up against conservative special interests that sow hate and discord in our communities. Jovanka has worked tirelessly to build a more caring, welcoming, inclusive, and sustainable East Bay.

“It's extraordinary. This is a celebration of democracy.

This means that big money doesn't always win, that ordinary people can defeat huge corporate power.”

SF Gate (November, 2014) Chevron’s $3 million backfires in Richmond election

Jovanka is fighting to deliver common sense, progressive results — for all of us:

Women, families, and children

As a State Senator, Jovanka will continue fighting for: Paid time off, free early childcare, and women's reproductive rights.

$100m funding

RESULTS

In 2014, Chevron Corporation and its right-wing allies spent an unprecedented $3+ million in an attempt to defeat Jovanka and influence Richmond City Council. Jovanka won — and kept receipts! She took Chevron to task over their rampant pollution and made them pay taxes. In total, she secured over $100 million to put toward our community, our youth, and our seniors.

Workers

$15 Min. Wage

RESULTS

On the Richmond City Council, Jovanka's “Fight for 15” won raises for Richmond workers. Her leadership earned widespread support, propelled Richmond's pro-worker legislation to become a model and inspiration for future Fight for 15 movements across the state, and beyond.

Fair Hiring

RESULTS

In 20XX, Jovanka's campaign to “Ban the Box” in Richmond forged the path forward to ban rampant unfair hiring practices, delivering equitable access to jobs for thousands of East Bay residents who were previously subject to discrimination.

As a State Senator, Jovanka will continue fighting for: Fair wages, power in our workplaces, and taxes on the rich.

Working homeowners and tenants

As a State Senator, Jovanka will continue fighting for: Housing that works for us as a human right, not the big landlords and developers.

Rent Control

RESULTS

In 2015, Jovanka earned praise from policy experts across the country for her victories for rent control in Richmond, the first successful movement of its type in California in decades. She also earned praise with her successes in stopping evictions of families and home owners, overwhelmed by predatory debt.

Our climate

As a State Senator, Jovanka will continue fighting for: A green New Deal for the East Bay, starting with seamless, green, zero-emission public transit, that we keep fare-free.

Transit Re-Opened

RESULTS

As AC Transit Director of Ward 1, Jovanka has successfully accomplished the re-opening of key Richmond bus-lines that provide transit resources to some of the most under-served community members, championing the rights of both transit workers & riders.

Our whole physical and mental health

As a State Senator, Jovanka will continue fighting for: Quality healthcare and education, as human rights, and free to all of us.

34 Years of Service

Jovanka is a mental healthcare professional, and her decades-long practice includes working with youth to overcome challenging circumstances, and has inspired and uplifted hundreds of East Bay residents. Many can personally attest to Jovanka's dedication to the health and well-being of our community. Once elected in Sacramento, she'll bring this and first-hand healthcare knowledge to advocate for everyone's right to free, quality, and life-long healthcare.
