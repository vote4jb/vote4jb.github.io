A trustworthy progressive leader

Director Jovanka Beckles has a proven track-record of progressive victories for the East Bay.

Jovanka Beckles is Director on the AC Transit Board. She is a movement organizer, a former two-term Richmond City Councilmember, a longtime leader in the Richmond Progressive Alliance, a children’s mental health professional, and a former candidate for California’s 15th State Assembly district. She is running for State Senate in District 7 to fight for the labor rights of workers and the system that the East Bay’s working people deserve.

In 2020, Jovanka lead a grassroots campaign for AC Transit with the boldest, greenest, and most progressive vision.
