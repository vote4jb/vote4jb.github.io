Jovanka fights for the working class. She fights for healthcare, good jobs, and progressive values for the East Bay. Learn more and join an event!

A trustworthy progressive leader

Jovanka Beckles has a proven track-record of down-to-earth, grassroots progressive victories for the East Bay.

“Jovanka Beckles is a movement organizer, a former two-term Richmond City Councilmember, a longtime leader in the Richmond Progressive Alliance, a children’s mental health professional, a former candidate for California’s 15th State Assembly district, and a democratic socialist in the tradition of Martin Luther King, Jr. She is running for State Senate in District 7 to fight for the labor rights of workers and the system that the East Bay’s working people deserve.”

In 2020, Jovanka lead a grassroots campaign for AC Transit with the boldest, greenest, and most progressive vision.

Jovanka Beckles has a decade-long track-record of progressive victories in the East Bay.
