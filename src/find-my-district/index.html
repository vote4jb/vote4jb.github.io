FAQ

What cities are in California Senate District 7 for March 2024?

Do you live in one of the following places? If so, you are in California Senate District 7 for March 2024.

  • Oakland
  • Berkeley
  • Richmond
  • Alameda
  • San Pablo
  • Hercules
  • El Cerrito
  • Albany
  • Pinole
  • Emeryville
  • Piedmont
  • El Sobrante
  • Rodeo
  • North Richmond
  • East Richmond Heights
  • Kensington

Additionally, Parts of San Leandro* are included in California Senate District 7.

Is Nancy Skinner running for re-election?

No. If your current State Senator is Nancy Skinner, then you are likely in California State Senate District 7. Be sure to vote March 2024!

Is this the same as California State Senate District 9?

Before the districts got new numbers in the 2021-2024 process, Nancy Skinner's district was called "District 9". This has been renumbered for 2024 due to general redistricting (internal rearranging of state boundaries). Regardless of which district you are in, be sure to vote March 2024!
